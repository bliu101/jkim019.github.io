How to adopt our hamsters

If you would like to adopt our hamsters, please fill out the form listed on the bottom of this home page. Before you adopt, you must meet the following requirements:

  • You must have AT LEAST A 40G SIZE ENCLOSURE
  • You must BE AVALIABLE TO PICKUP from our business
  • You must have FILLED OUT THE FORM
  • You must NOT USE THE HAMSTER FOR YOUR OWN BREEDING OR FEEDING

If you have any additional questions, please contact us.
